2025 Conference Series

In order of attendance, these are the conferences that I attended in 2025.
  1. Spring 2025 Energy Intermarket Surveillance Group (EISG) - Boston, MA
  2. SAS Innovate 2025 - Orlando, FL
  3. Western Users of SAS Software (WUSS) 2025 - Los Angeles, CA
ISO New England (IESO) organized this trip.

This picture was taken outside of the Seaport Hotel in Boston, MA. ISO New England's Market Monitoring group organized this conference. I believe that most market monitoring entities sent a contingent (of at least a few people). I'm pictured on the far left. This really has been a great community of people to be part of. Few times in my life have I gotten to be immersed in such a large "think tank" of genuinely curious people.
